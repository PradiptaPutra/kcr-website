| **Vercel Pro** | **$20 (~Rp 320.000)** | **Recommended for Growth.** Removes commercial limits and adds deep analytics. |

### What if we stay on the Free Tier?
If the website traffic remains moderate (typical for a corporate B2B site), the **Free Tier is 100% capable** of hosting the site. It will remain fast, secure, and accessible. The upgrade to "Pro" is only necessary if:
1.  Traffic exceeds 100GB/month (~50k+ visitors).
2.  The company requires advanced visitor analytics or team collaboration features.
3.  Vercel officially requests a commercial upgrade for the business domain.
